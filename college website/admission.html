Admissions


Admission Poster :-


Eligibility :-

(a) Diploma in Engineering (3 year) in relevant branch with minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST candidates) in aggregate.
OR
Degree in science (B.Sc.) with mathematics as subject and with minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST candidates & GKV Graduates) in aggregate.

Admissions in all 4 branches are on the councelling of JEE Mains Examination (JOSAA). Few seat which can not be filled-out through the councelling are filled through the councelling of the University itself.
How to Apply :-

Application form and Information Brochure for entrance examination can be obtained from the office of Dean, Faculty of Engineering & Technology or office of Registrar Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya by paying cash of Rs.1000.
Application form can also be downloaded from University website www.gkv.ac.in. Downloaded duly filled application form along with demand draft of Rs 1000/- in favour of “Registrar, Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya” payable at Haridwar, can be submitted at the office of Dean, Faculty of Engineering & Technology, Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar, 10 KM Haridwar Delhi Road, Near Shani Dev Mandir, Shradhanand Puram, Bahadrabad, Haridwar (Uttarakhand).
The candidates appearing in the final year of qualifying examination are also eligible to apply. Filled in application form for entrance examination may be submitted to the office of Dean, Faculty of Engineering & Technology.
